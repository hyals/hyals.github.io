About this work

Synopsis

Hya has spent sixteen years believing his very existence brings sorrow to others. When he volunteers for the Awakening, it’s not for glory but for atonement: a desperate attempt to earn a place in a world that has always rejected him. But the Awakening forces him to face his “other-self,” a manifestation of his past sins, and each surge of aura chips away at his lifespan. And while the divide collapses and adults march toward bloodshed, they all must decide whether to embrace the monster within or watch their loved ones perish. Either path will demand their lives—one slowly, the other all at once.
